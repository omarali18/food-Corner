Global expansion of markets

Pizza sales gross about $37 billion annually in the US. While the market in the West may be approaching saturation, it is now mushrooming in the far East. It is eaten almost everywhere. Former Premier Gorbachev advertised pizza for Pizza Hut at a Moscow restaurant. The Schwan Food Company currently exports frozen pizza to Russia. In the expression, “As American as apple pie” it has been suggested that “apple” be replaced by “pizza.” There is evidence to support this revision.

About Me

Pizza can be traced back to its origin in the Persian Empire around 500 BC when flat bread was topped with cheese and dates. Tomato was added by the Romans (Qarooni et al., 1992). Pizza coated with spices, oils and sesame seeds is still popular in the Middle-East (maneesh). Modern pizza is a firmly established food in the west, with the crust produced by a sheeting and die-cut procedure, hot or cold pressing, or shaping by hand. Dough retardation (where dough is held at refrigerator temperatures) is often used in fast-food franchises to enable timely meeting of customer orders.
